Key Drivers

Drivers of Change

The future of work is being shaped by powerful forces. Understanding these drivers is essential for navigating the evolving landscape.

  • Advancements in AI, big data, robotics, and automation are the most significant drivers. Technology is increasingly used to augment human work but is also automating many routine tasks.

  • Efforts to mitigate and adapt to climate change are creating high demand for "green" jobs in areas like renewable energy and environmental engineering.

  • Aging populations in higher-income economies are increasing demand for healthcare, while growing working-age populations in lower-income economies fuel demand for educators.

  • There is a growing focus on employee well-being, flexibility, diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI), with hybrid work models becoming a permanent feature.
